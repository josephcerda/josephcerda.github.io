Qwitcher Grypen typeface was created by Robert E. Leuschke out of St. Louis, Missouri. Qwitcher Grypen is a letterform that comes from using an architectural ruling pen; it is a casual brush script with a bit of an edge. It feels like you can really relate to this font, I was very quick to draw a comparison with Harry Potter movies having a very relatable feeling with its characters.
